Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician who has served as the 14th Prime Minister of India since 26 May 2014.Modi was the chief minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a right wing Hindu nationalist paramilitary volunteer organisation. He is the longest-serving prime minister outside the Indian National Congress.

Early life and education

Modi had infrequently worked as a child in his father's tea business on the Vadnagar railway station platform, according to Modi and his neighbours.
